Hope Restoration Association is a Jordanian non-profit initiative launched under royal directives to support individuals with limb loss, particularly in Gaza. In collaboration with the Royal Medical Services, the Jordan Hashemite Charity Organization, and Hakeem (Electronic Health Solutions), the association provides advanced prosthetic limbs using modern technology that enables fitting within just one hour. Over 415 individuals have benefited so far, and the association aims to reach 16,000 beneficiaries in Gaza. The initiative was recently highlighted at the 3rd Global Disability Summit in Berlin, where King Abdullah II viewed its future plans to expand globally and build professional capacity.
